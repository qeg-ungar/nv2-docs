Sample Mounting
===============

This procedure covers mounting the diamond sample onto the PCB and installing the PCB assembly
onto the microscope stage.

Required Materials
------------------

- Diamond sample
- PCB with antenna
- Bonding agent (crystal bond / silver paint / *specify*)
- Tweezers, clean-room swabs
- Isopropanol, acetone (for cleaning)
- Microscope for inspection

Safety
------

.. caution::

   Handle the diamond with clean tweezers only — fingerprint oils contaminate the surface and
   increase background fluorescence.

Procedure
---------

1. PCB Preparation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1. Clean the PCB bonding area with IPA and allow to dry.
2. Inspect under microscope for debris or damage to the antenna trace.

2. Diamond Surface Preparation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1. Clean the diamond with acetone, then IPA, then blow dry with N₂.
2. Inspect under UV or white-light illumination for surface debris.

5. Installing on the Microscope Stage
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1. Mount PCB onto the XYZ piezo stage.
2. Connect SMA cables from PCB antenna to MW chain.
3. Connect heater/thermometer leads to temperature controller.
4. Verify electrical continuity on all connections.

6. Initial Verification
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Camera image shows diamond surface in focus
- APD counts rise when laser is on and sample is in focal plane
- MW reflection (S11) looks reasonable on network analyzer or power meter
- Temperature reads correctly on PID controller

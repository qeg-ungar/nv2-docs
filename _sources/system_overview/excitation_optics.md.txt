# Excitation Optics

The excitation path delivers 532 nm light to the diamond sample for NV center spin initialization and readout.

## Components

- **Experiment laser** — 532 nm DPSS laser
- **AOM (Acousto-Optic Modulator)** — fast optical switching and intensity control; driven by QM OPX TTL
- **Excitation optics** — beam shaping, steering mirrors, dichroic, and objective
